Private Pool Parties

Professional lifeguard services for private pool parties, ensuring the safety of all guests while allowing hosts to relax and enjoy the event.

Corporate Events

Ensure employee and guest safety at company pool events with our professional lifeguard services, allowing your team to focus on team-building and enjoyment.

HOA & Community Events

Swimming Lessons

We work with all types and levels of swimmers from 3 to 99 years old. Our individualized approach ensures comfort and rapid progress.

Children's Lessons

Adult Lessons

Personalized swimming instruction for adults of all skill levels, from beginners overcoming water anxiety to advanced swimmers refining their technique.

Special Needs Swimming

Specialized instruction for individuals with physical, cognitive, or sensory challenges, provided with patience, expertise, and adaptive techniques.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

What areas do you serve?

We provide lifeguard services and swimming lessons throughout the greater Conejo Valley area, including Thousand Oaks, Westlake Village, Agoura Hills, Newbury Park, Moorpark, and Camarillo.

How far in advance should I book lifeguard services?

We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance during the California swim season (May-September) to ensure availability. For peak times or large events, booking 3-4 weeks ahead is ideal.

What age groups do you teach?

We work with swimmers of all ages, from 3 to 99 years old and beyond! Our teaching methods are adjusted based on age, ability, and individual learning styles.

Do you provide the pool for swimming lessons?

No, we do not provide a pool setting. We come to your home or community pool for both lifeguard services and swimming lessons, allowing you to enjoy the privacy and comfort of your own environment.

What qualifications do your lifeguards have?

All our lifeguards are fully certified with Red Cross Lifeguard Training, CPR/AED for the Professional Rescuer, and First Aid. Many have additional certifications and years of experience in various aquatic environments.

How long does it take to learn to swim?

Learning progress varies greatly depending on the individual's age, natural ability, comfort in water, and consistency of lessons. Most children can become water-safe within 8-10 lessons, while adults typically require 5-15 lessons to develop basic swimming skills.
