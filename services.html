Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and booking process.

What areas do you serve?

We provide lifeguard services and swimming lessons throughout Southern California, including the Conejo Valley, Thousand Oaks, Westlake Village, Agoura Hills, Newbury Park, Moorpark, and Camarillo. Contact us for specific service area confirmation.

How far in advance should I book lifeguard services?

We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance during the California swim season (May-September) to ensure availability. For peak times or large events, booking 3-4 weeks ahead is ideal for the best service guarantee.

What age groups do you teach?

We work with swimmers of all ages, from 3 to 99 years old and beyond! Our teaching methods are carefully adjusted based on age, ability, learning style, and individual comfort levels to ensure the best possible experience.

Do you provide the pool for swimming lessons?

No, we do not provide a pool setting. We come to your home or community pool for both lifeguard services and swimming lessons, allowing you to enjoy the privacy and comfort of your own familiar environment.

What qualifications do your lifeguards have?

All our lifeguards are fully certified with Red Cross Lifeguard Training, CPR/AED for the Professional Rescuer, and First Aid. Many have additional certifications and years of experience in various aquatic environments and emergency situations.

How long does it take to learn to swim?

Learning progress varies greatly depending on the individual's age, natural ability, comfort in water, and consistency of lessons. Most children can become water-safe within 8-10 lessons, while adults typically require 5-15 lessons to develop basic swimming skills and confidence.
