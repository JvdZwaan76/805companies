About Us

Trusted Water Safety Experts

Since 2016, 805 LifeGuard has been committed to teaching water safety and providing professional lifeguard services throughout Southern California. Our mission is to create safe, enjoyable water experiences for everyone.

We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy the water safely, which is why we offer personalized swim lessons for all ages and abilities, as well as professional lifeguard services for private events and community facilities.

Certified Professionals

All our lifeguards are certified and undergo continuous training

Everyone is family
