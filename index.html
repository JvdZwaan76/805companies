About 805 LifeGuard

Since 2016, 805 LifeGuard has been committed to teaching water safety and providing professional lifeguard services in the greater Conejo Valley area. Our mission is to create safe, enjoyable water experiences for everyone.

We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy the water safely, which is why we offer personalized swim lessons for all ages and abilities, as well as professional lifeguard services for private events and community facilities.

Do small things with great love

We put our heart into everything we do, ensuring each lesson and service is delivered with care, attention to detail, and genuine dedication to your safety and success.

Everyone is family

We treat every client like family, providing personalized attention and creating a comfortable, welcoming learning environment where everyone can thrive.

Whatever it takes

We're committed to going above and beyond to ensure the safety and success of our clients, adapting our approach to meet individual needs and circumstances.
