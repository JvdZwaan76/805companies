About 805 LifeGuard

Since 2016, 805 LifeGuard has been committed to teaching water safety and providing professional lifeguard services in the greater Conejo Valley area.

We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy the water safely, which is why we offer personalized swim lessons for all ages and abilities, as well as professional lifeguard services for private events.
